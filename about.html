About GM Studio

Founded by Gulshan Makhija · Mumbai, India

Your Portrait Photo
Gulshan Makhija — Founder & Lead Photographer

We photograph the light no one else waits for

GM Studio was founded by Gulshan Makhija, a Mumbai-based photographer with a deep passion for storytelling through the lens. What started as a personal obsession with capturing Mumbai's ever-changing light has grown into a full-service creative studio trusted by families, brands, and couples across India.

Gulshan's eye was shaped by years spent wandering the city's streets — from the chaotic colour of Crawford Market to the quiet grandeur of the Gateway at dawn. That same instinct for finding beauty in unexpected places now drives every project GM Studio takes on.

Today GM Studio's work spans commercial product campaigns, intimate wedding films, dramatic landscapes, and documentary street work — each project treated with the same care and intention, whether the client is a Fortune 500 brand or a couple celebrating their first anniversary.

We shoot with patience. We edit with restraint. And we believe the best photograph is the one you almost didn't take.
