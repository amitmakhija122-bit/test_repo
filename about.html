About me

Mumbai · India

Your Portrait Photo
GM Studio

I photograph the light no one else waits for

I grew up in the narrow lanes of Dadar, where every monsoon afternoon turned the streets into a mirror. My father had an old Yashica film camera, and I'd carry it everywhere — not to take pictures, but just to look through the viewfinder and pretend the world was a film set.

That curiosity never left. After studying design at MIT Institute of Design, Pune, I apprenticed with editorial photographers in Delhi before returning to Mumbai with a single purpose: to build a body of work that could only have been made here.

Today my work spans landscapes shot from Ladakh to Lakshadweep, street portraiture from Dharavi and Colaba, commercial product work for brands like Tata CLiQ and Nykaa, and wedding films that families watch on anniversaries decades later.

I shoot with patience. I edit with restraint. And I believe the best photograph is the one you almost didn't take.
