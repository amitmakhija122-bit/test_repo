About GM Studio

Light is my
language

Based in Mumbai, Gulshan Makhija has spent over a decade chasing light across the city's rooftops, ghats, and hidden alleys. GM Studio's work spans commercial product photography to intimate wedding stories — each frame crafted with intent and patience.

We believe a photograph doesn't just capture a moment; it preserves a feeling. Whether it's the blue hour over the Arabian Sea or a bride's nervous smile, We look for the quiet emotion beneath the surface.
