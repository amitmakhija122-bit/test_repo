About GM Studio

Light is our

Founded by Gulshan Makhija, GM Studio has spent over a decade chasing light across Mumbai's rooftops, ghats, and hidden alleys. Our work spans commercial product photography to intimate wedding stories.

We believe a photograph doesn't just capture a moment; it preserves a feeling. Whether it's the blue hour over the Arabian Sea or a bride's nervous smile, we look for the quiet emotion beneath the surface.
